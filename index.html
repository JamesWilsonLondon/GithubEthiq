What is ETHIQ

Not an agency pitch

ETHIQ is a specialist search partner for ambitious tech product companies. We hire engineers, data and AI profiles, and product leaders for teams that treat hiring as product work, not admin. We embed with founders and product leaders, sit close to the roadmap, and bias for long term impact over short term headcount.

ETHIQ is not an agency. It behaves like an extension of your product team. No CV blasts, no pressure selling, no lock in contracts. If that sounds right, great. If it does not, that is fine too.

How we evaluate engineers

For founders and candidates
  • Context first: understanding the problem and constraints before code.
  • Reasoning over puzzles: how you arrive at an approach matters more than trivia.
  • Impact trajectory: what you shipped and unblocked, not only titles held.
  • Collaboration: PRs, docs and feedback that other people enjoy working with.
  • No LeetCode style tests and no surprise whiteboard gotchas.

Engineering values we look for

Patterns we keep seeing in great hires
  • Ownership loops: closing the loop from bug or idea through to deployed change.
  • Strong debugging: curiosity, systems thinking, and a low drama approach to outages.
  • Leverage: picks tools and approaches that buy future speed, not just today.
  • Clear writing: can explain tradeoffs in plain language to non engineers.

What candidates get before interviews

No one walks in blind
  • Context on the product, team and runway in normal human language.
  • A clear outline of the interview stages and what each round is testing.
  • Preparation notes tailored to the company, not generic interview tips.
  • Honest notes on risks and unknowns so they can make a real decision.

Pricing philosophy

Simple and predictable

We price searches based on seniority and complexity, not on who negotiates harder on the day. Same effort means the same fee. No retainers by default and no long lock in contracts. If we are not adding value, you should be able to stop.

Working with ETHIQ

For teams who care about hiring

If you want a partner who will think about hiring with the same care you put into shipping product, we should talk. If what you need is a steady stream of loosely qualified CVs at speed, we are probably not the right fit. Either way, no hard feelings.

We only work with a small set of active partners at any one time so that we can stay close to the work. Search capacity is capped on purpose.
