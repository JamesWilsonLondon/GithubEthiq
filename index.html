What is ETHIQ

Not an agency pitch

ETHIQ is a specialist search partner for ambitious tech product companies across the UK, Europe and the US. We work with a selected group of partners at any one time, stay close to the teams and the roadmap, and treat hiring as part of the product work. No spray and pray CV blasts, no pressure selling, no lock-in contracts. If our way of working fits, great. If it does not, that is fine too.
